Metrodroid Stops Data Collection

This website crowd-sources information from users like you about what unknown stop IDs exist on your public transit smartcard. This information enhances Metrodroid, an open source Android app for reading public transit smartcards around the world.

I'm currently collecting information from Translink Go cards, used in Brisbane and South Eastern Queensland, Australia.
